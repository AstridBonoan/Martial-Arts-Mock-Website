MMA and Fitness For Kids and Teens

Kids Boxing and Fitness

The Kids Fitness program is for children between the ages of 5-12. The program is great discipline, focus and confidence. The class consists of many fitness drills and lots of excercise. The children who attend the program will also learn self-defense (boxing).

Teens Fitness (13-17)

This program is very similar to an MMA program. It will consist of striking and grappling training. At this gym, we truly believe that our youth should be able to defend themselves. So we train our students to be comfortable with striking from standing as well as defend themselves on the ground. This program has many benefits such as focus, discipline, confidence and fitness. Classes will teach the following:
  • Boxing
  • BJJ
  • MMA
  • Fitness
